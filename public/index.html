About Me

I am focused on creating clean, responsive, and effective React web apps.

Growing up, I spent most of my time online. My love for computers and the internet naturally translated into a passion for software engineering and web development. By programming during my formal education and in my spare time, I learned to embrace a developer mindset. I know that any problem can be overcome through persistence and a bit of trial and error.

I find modern web development exciting. My experience includes working with the MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) stack, Git version control, utilizing AWS for cloud deployment, and implementing an Agile development process.

In my free time, I enjoy creating new projects and experimenting with new ideas and tools. I engage with my creative side through software like Photoshop and Blender. When not staring at pixels, I like to get out by going on road trips or hiking the many beautiful parks in Massachusetts.
